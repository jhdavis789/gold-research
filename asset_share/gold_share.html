Gold as a share of the US asset universe

The value of all above-ground gold, measured against three progressively wider US asset bases. Same numerator throughout; only the denominator grows. Toggle the base below.

Underlying data

Numerator: world above-ground gold stock (WGC anchors, interpolated) × annual-avg LBMA $/oz. Denominators (US, year-end): corp equities NCBEILQ027S+FBCELLQ027S; corp+foreign bonds ASCFBL; federal public debt GFDEBTN (gross); M1SL (Dec); owner-occupied real estate BOGZ1FL155035015Q. Gold is a single global market priced worldwide; there is no clean US-only above-ground-gold series, so the numerator is the global stock against the US asset base. M1 was redefined May 2020 (savings deposits folded in) — a one-time level break in the financial-asset and total bases. Figures revised by source publishers.
